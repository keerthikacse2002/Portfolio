Smart street lighting system




A street light system using IoT (Internet of Things) can enhance energy efficiency and safety by automatically adjusting light intensity based on real-time data such as traffic flow, weather conditions, and time of day. This smart system can also enable remote monitoring and control, reducing maintenance costs and improving overall street lighting management.
